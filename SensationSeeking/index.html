How Much of a Sensation Seeker Are You?

Take this short quiz to find out whether you exhibit the traits of a sensation-seeker

What is the sensation seeking personality trait?

Sensation-seeking is a consistent pattern of behavior where people prefer strong sensory stimulation and a diversity of experiences/sensations. The search for these feelings and experiences often (but not always) involves some element of risk.

There are 4 different identifiable types of sensation-seeking:

  • Thrill and adventure seeking: people who take an interest in extreme sports like skydiving or white water rafting.
  • Experience seeking: people who seek out unfamiliar and complex environmental stimulation e.g. by meeting new people or travelling on their own.
  • Disinhibition: people who enjoy losing their inhibitions, such as by getting drunk and using drugs.
  • Boredom Susceptibility: people who dislike routines and pursue change for change's sake.
