Cancellations

In keeping with Music Teachers National Association guidelines, lessons cancelled less than 24 hours in advance (except in emergency) are non-refundable.

Weather

In the event of snow, please contact us if you cannot come. We don't follow school closure procedure, as sometimes the roads are clear by afternoon. If our driveway is inaccessible, we will call you.
