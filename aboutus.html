About Us

Donna Mulzet Beech

  • Doctor of Musical Arts (Piano Performance), The Catholic University of America, Washington, D.C.
  • Master of Music (Piano Performance), West Chester University, West Chester, P.A.
  • Bachelor of Science in Music Education, West Chester University, West Chester, P.A.
  • Member:
    • National Piano Guild, American College of Musicians
    • Music Teachers National Association
    • Pennsylvania MTA
    • Main Line MTA
    • Delaware State MTA
    • Northern Delaware MTA
  • Over 3 decades of private teaching experience
  • Performances as soloist and chamber musician
  • Adjudicator in PA, NJ, DE, MD
  • Organizer and conductor, Annual Jonathan Beech Memorial Concert (see link below)

Martin Beech

  • Master of Music (Violin Performance) West Chester University
  • B.S. in Music Education, West Chester University
  • Member:
    • Pennsylvania Music Educators Association
    • National Association for Music Education
    • American String Teachers Association
    • American Federation of Musicians
  • Over four decades of private teaching experience
  • 39 years as orchestra director and string teacher in Kennett Consolidated School District
  • Martin has performed with many area orchestras and chamber groups over the years
  • He is currently a member of the Delaware Symphony Orchestra, Kennett Symphony Orchestra and The Kennett Trio
